About All Minnesota

The Story

All Minnesota exists to bring greater Minnesota together to help communities of need. We believe everyone deserves access to nourishing food and that community meals build connection and dignity. This work is rooted in partnership with indigenous-led initiatives, community organizations, and local volunteers who make it possible.

The Partnership

We work with tribal nations, community organizations, and partners across Minnesota to fund and coordinate meal preparation and distribution. Together we get nourishing, culturally rooted meals to communities of need across greater Minnesota.

The Team

Keith Secola and other key leaders bring decades of commitment to community and indigenous food sovereignty. Our team and volunteers are the heart of All Minnesota — all in this together.

Our Commitment

We are committed to transparency, respect for tribal sovereignty, and building lasting relationships with the communities we serve. Every dollar raised and every volunteer hour goes toward feeding and connecting our neighbors across greater Minnesota.
