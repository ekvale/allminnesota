About All Minnesota

The Story

All Minnesota exists to bring the whole state together in support of communities hit hardest by federal actions. We believe everyone deserves access to nourishing food and that community meals build connection and dignity. This work is rooted in partnership with indigenous-led initiatives, community organizations, and local volunteers who make it possible.

The Partnership

We work with tribal nations, community organizations, and partners across Minnesota to fund and coordinate meal preparation and distribution. Together we get nourishing, culturally rooted meals to the people and places that need them most.

The Team

Keith Secola and other key leaders bring decades of commitment to community and indigenous food sovereignty. Our team and volunteers are the heart of All Minnesota — all in this together.

Our Commitment

We are committed to transparency, respect for tribal sovereignty, and building lasting relationships with the communities hit hardest by federal actions. Every dollar raised and every volunteer hour goes toward feeding and connecting our neighbors across Minnesota.
