About All Minnesota

The Story

All Minnesota exists to support indigenous-led community meal distribution across the state. We believe everyone deserves access to nourishing food and that community meals build connection and dignity. This work is rooted in partnership with Native chefs, tribal nations, and local volunteers who make it possible.

The Partnership

We work alongside Sean Sherman (The Sioux Chef) and the Indigenous Food Lab, along with tribal nations and community partners across Minnesota. Together we fund and coordinate meal preparation and distribution so that more people can share in culturally rooted, nutritious meals.

The Team

Keith Secola and other key leaders bring decades of commitment to community and indigenous food sovereignty. Our team and volunteers are the heart of All Minnesota — all in this together.

Our Commitment

We are committed to transparency, respect for tribal sovereignty, and building lasting relationships with the communities we serve. Every dollar raised and every volunteer hour goes toward feeding and connecting our neighbors.
